Character Generator


RPGs, or role playing games, are one of the most popular types of activity Americans,
both kids and Adults, engage in. RPGs come in a number of forms, from complex video
games like skyrim to pencil and paper setups such as Dungeon and Dragons. In my
opinion, the first and most important challenge in any RPG is what character you want
to build. Often a character is a mix of realistic elements from our personal lives and
fantastical elements that we aspire or dream of having. There can sometimes be a sort
of writer's block in the construction of these characters.

The purpose of this site is to provide inspiration for any character you wish to make.
First, I have provided a random avatar generator to help visualize a potential look
for your character. Second, I have provided a random activity generator to inspire
possible things your character might do. Have fun and let your imagination guide you!
